# BBEG Battle Ideas Index

This index catalogues epic "Big Bad Evil Guy" battle sessions for The Arena campaign, organized by difficulty tier.

## Low-Tier (Levels 3-4)
- **[[wings_of_fire]]** - White Dragon Wyrmling variant (see Multi-Tier Dragon Variants section)

## Mid-Tier (Levels 5-7)
- **[[wings_of_fire]]** - Young Dragon battles with multiple chromatic/metallic options
- **[[lords_of_lichdom]]** - Twin necromancers with phylactery mechanics (Level 7-9)
- **[[mind_flayers_exhibition]]** - Illithid psychic supremacy challenge (Level 7-9)

## High-Tier (Levels 8-10)
- **[[demon_princes_wager]]** - Glabrezu and demonic horde (Level 8-10)
- **[[iron_colossus_prime]]** - Modular war-golem with elemental cores (Level 8-10)
- **[[crimson_waltz]]** - Vampire lord gothic horror (Level 9-11)
- **[[archmage_ascendant]]** - Spellcaster supremacy with Shield Guardians (Level 9-11)

## Epic-Tier (Levels 10-14)
- **[[all_seeing_nightmare]]** - Beholder and anti-magic tactics (Level 10-12)
- **[[avatar_of_the_storm]]** - Storm Giant environmental chaos (Level 11-13)
- **[[titans_gauntlet]]** - Bound titan escape scenario (Level 12-14)
- **[[wings_of_fire]]** - Adult/Ancient Dragon variants (see Multi-Tier Dragon Variants)

## BBEG Type Quick Reference

### Spellcasters
- Archmage (Archmage Ascendant)
- Necromancers (Lords of Lichdom)
- Vampire (Crimson Waltz - has spell-like abilities)

### Aberrations
- Beholder (All-Seeing Nightmare)
- Mind Flayer (Mind Flayer's Exhibition)

### Dragons
- Multiple chromatic/metallic options (Wings of Fire)

### Fiends
- Demon (Demon Prince's Wager)

### Giants
- Storm Giant (Avatar of the Storm)
- Titan/Empyrean (Titan's Gauntlet)

### Undead
- Vampire (Crimson Waltz)
- Necromancers with undead army (Lords of Lichdom)

### Constructs
- War Golem (Iron Colossus Prime)

## Mechanical Features Cross-Reference

### Phylactery/Resurrection Mechanics
- Lords of Lichdom (necromancer phylacteries)
- Vampire's coffin escape (Crimson Waltz)

### Modular/Phased Combat
- Iron Colossus Prime (destroyable elemental cores)
- Titan's Gauntlet (chain-breaking phases)
- Wings of Fire (dragon age categories)

### Environmental Hazards
- Avatar of the Storm (lightning strikes, wind)
- All-Seeing Nightmare (anti-magic cone)
- Mind Flayer's Exhibition (gravity shifts)
- Titan's Gauntlet (earthquake, chasms)

### Charm/Mind Control
- Vampire (Crimson Waltz)
- Mind Flayer (Mind Flayer's Exhibition)
- Demon (Demon Prince's Wager - pact offers)

### Flying Enemies
- Wings of Fire (dragon flight)
- All-Seeing Nightmare (beholder aerial mobility)
- Avatar of the Storm (Air Elementals, Aarakocra)

### Summoning/Reinforcements
- Demon Prince's Wager (portal spawns)
- Lords of Lichdom (animated undead)
- Avatar of the Storm (call lightning, air elementals)
