Track Favor per combatant.

**Gain Favor:**

- A **successful** Panache roll grants **+1 Favor**.

**Lose Favor:**

- A **failed** Panache roll with a natural result of 5 **or lower** (before modifiers) causes **–1 Favor**.

### **Hot Crowd**

After **3 consecutive successful** Panache rolls, the crowd becomes **Hot** for that combatant:

- All further **with Panache** rolls gain **+2**.
- Hot status ends immediately when the combatant **loses any Favor**.

# Crowds Favor

## **1 Favor Options**

### **Surge of Applause**

**Free action, Spend before a roll.**

Gain **+1d4** to the roll.

### **Flair Boost**

Add **+1d4** to any attack roll, skill check, or save (declared before or after rolling).

### **Flash Step**

Move **10 feet** without provoking opportunity attacks.

### **Show-Off Guard**

Gain **+1 AC** until the start of your next turn.

### **Taunting Flourish**

Force a creature within 15 ft. to make a **Wisdom save** or have **disadvantage on its next attack against anyone but you**.

### **Eyecatcher**

Give an ally you can see **+1** on their next attack roll or check.

(Tiny but flavorful — “the crowd shifts, your ally takes the cue.”)

### **Recover Your Footing**

Stand from prone using **no movement** (bonus action).

### **Flash Recuperation**

Gain **temp HP = your proficiency bonus**.

### **The Wink**

After dealing damage, regain **1d4 HP** (“the crowd lifts your spirits”).

---

## **2 Favor Options**

### Moral Support

Take the **Help** action as a **bonus action**.

### Turn the Knife

Reroll **damage that** you just rolled.

### **Light in Their Eyes**

After an enemy hits you, force them to reroll the attack.

### **The Chant Rises**

You gain **advantage on your next saving throw**.

### Salt in the wound

Add **+1d6** to a single attack **damage roll** after you hit.

### **Bold Stance**

Until the end of your next turn:

- You can’t be **pushed or knocked prone**
- Opportunity attacks against you are at **disadvantage**

### **Sizzle Sniper**

Your next melee or ranged attack **ignores half cover and three-quarters cover**.

### **Stage Fright**

As a bonus action, choose a creature within 30 ft. It must succeed on a **Wisdom save** or it cannot take reactions until the start of your next turn.

### **Swagger Step**

Gain **advantage on your next Acrobatics or Athletics check**.

### **Roaring Momentum**

**After hitting or succeeding**, immediately move up to **half your speed** without provoking opportunity attacks.

---

## **3 Favor Mid-Tier Showmanship Uses**

### **Rally the Arena**

Grant **+1d6 to all allies' next attack roll** (one-time).

Only triggers once per round per arena rules.

### **Tankin’ Time**

As a bonus action, give yourself **temporary HP = your proficiency bonus × 2**.

---

## **High-Tier “Signature Move” Uses (expensive, flashy, unforgettable)**

### **Turn the Tide (5 Favor)**

Once per encounter:

**Free action**: You _automatically succeed_ on a death saving throw and stand up with 1 HP.

### **Finishers Spotlight (5 Favor)**

Declare at the start of your turn:

For this turn, **your attacks deal +10 damage** (flat).

### **The Arena’s Blessing (6 Favor)**

The crowd starts chanting your name.

For the next **3 rounds**:

- All **with Panache** rolls gain **advantage** (stacking with “Hot Crowd” rules as normal).
- If you drop to 0 HP, you drop to 1 instead (once).
