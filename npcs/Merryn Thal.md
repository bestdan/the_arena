
**The Smile Behind the Mask**

**Race/Class:** Half-Elf (Rogue/Entertainer) woman. 
**Appearance:** Dashing, flamboyant, wears half-masks and bright silks even in the bloodstained sand.  
**Personality:** Charismatic, self-absorbed, and always scheming.  
**Background:** A performer from the capital who volunteered for the arena for fame. Turns out the glamour was a lie, but the cameras still love her.  

**Hook:** Miren becomes a mouthpiece for the “audience’s will.” She’s favored by sponsors and starts getting special treatment—maybe even missions. She can help the party gain influence… or become their PR enemy if they cross him.

**Skills**: Great shot with a bow
