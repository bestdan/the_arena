# Snak's Shop - Session 6

*"A hunt, eh? You'll need speed, soft hands, and something to deal with those thorns. Let me show you what'll help."*

---

## Consumables (One-Time Use)

### Potion of Speed - 200 GP
*"Rare find. Doubles your speed for 1 minute, extra action each turn. Perfect for chasing something FAST. Side effect hits after, but the hunt'll be over by then."*

**Effect:**
- Action to drink
- Duration: 1 minute (10 rounds)
- Double your speed
- +2 AC
- Advantage on Dexterity saves
- Extra action each turn (Attack, Dash, Disengage, Hide, or Use Object only)
- **After Effect:** Lethargy—can't move or take actions until end of next turn after potion ends

---

### Oil of Slipperiness - 80 GP
*"Slick yourself up. Hard to grab, hard to hold. Good if you're carrying the prize and don't want it stolen."*

**Effect:**
- Action to apply to self or object
- Duration: 8 hours
- Grapple attempts against you have disadvantage
- Advantage on checks to escape grapples or restraints
- Disadvantage on checks to hold onto objects (careful with the Hummingwyrm!)

---

### Potion of Animal Friendship - 60 GP
*"Makes beasts see you as a friend. Might calm that Hummingwyrm down, make it easier to hold. Worth a shot."*

**Effect:**
- Action to drink
- Cast *animal friendship* (DC 13 Wisdom save)
- Affects beasts you can see within 30 feet
- Duration: 24 hours or until you/allies harm the creature
- Calmed creatures won't attack you

---

### Healing Potion (2 for 100 GP) - 100 GP
*"Standard issue. 2d4+2 healing. Those thorns'll cut you up—you'll need it."*

**Effect:**
- Action to drink or feed to another
- Restore 2d4+2 hit points
- **Quantity:** 2 potions

---

### Greater Healing Potion - 150 GP
*"Better medicine. 4d4+4 healing. For when the rivals get serious."*

**Effect:**
- Action to drink or feed to another
- Restore 4d4+4 hit points

---

### Antitoxin (2 doses) - 100 GP
*"Those thorn bushes are poisonous. This'll help. Advantage on poison saves, can end poisoning if you're already stuck."*

**Effect:**
- Action to drink
- Advantage on saving throws vs. poison for 1 hour
- If poisoned, one saving throw to end the condition
- **Quantity:** 2 doses

---

### Smokebomb (3 for 40 GP) - 40 GP
*"Block line of sight, confuse the rivals. 10-foot smoke cloud for 1 minute. Good for quick escapes or hiding your position."*

**Effect:**
- Action to throw (30-foot range)
- Creates heavily obscured area in 10-foot radius
- Duration: 1 minute
- **Quantity:** 3 bombs

---

### Tanglefoot Bag (2 for 50 GP) - 50 GP
*"Throw it at someone's feet. DC 13 Strength save or they're stuck—speed zero for 1 minute. Slows down rivals chasing you."*

**Effect:**
- Action to throw (20-foot range)
- Ranged attack against creature or ground space
- Target makes DC 13 Strength save
- **Failure:** Speed reduced to 0 for 1 minute (can use action to attempt DC 13 Strength check to break free)
- **Quantity:** 2 bags

---

### Bag of Caltrops - 5 GP
*"Classic. Spread 'em in chokepoints. Slows movement, deals damage. Use 'em to control the arena."*

**Effect:**
- Action to spread in 5-foot square area
- Creatures moving through take 1 piercing damage
- Speed halved until they leave the area
- DC 15 Perception to spot before stepping on them

---

### Ball Bearings (bag of 1000) - 5 GP
*"Spill 'em, create chaos. 10-foot square becomes difficult terrain, DC 10 Dex save or prone. Trips up rivals nicely."*

**Effect:**
- Action to spread in 10-foot square area
- Area becomes difficult terrain
- Creatures moving through must make DC 10 Dexterity save or fall prone

---

### Alchemist's Fire (2 flasks) - 50 GP
*"1d4 fire damage, keeps burning. Useful for area denial, but don't hit the Hummingwyrm with it—you'll kill the poor thing."*

**Effect:**
- Action to throw (20-foot range)
- Ranged attack: 1d4 fire damage on hit
- Target takes 1d4 fire damage at start of each turn until they use action to extinguish
- **Warning:** Can kill the Hummingwyrm if used carelessly!
- **Quantity:** 2 flasks

## Equipment & Gear

### Padded Gloves - 30 GP
*"Thick leather, reinforced padding. Helps you hold delicate things without crushing them. +2 to Animal Handling when handling fragile creatures."*

**Effect:**
- +2 bonus to Animal Handling checks to hold or calm Small or Tiny creatures
- Reduces risk of accidentally harming creatures you're holding
- Does not stack with magical bonuses

---

### Thorn-Resistant Cloak - 60 GP
*"Treated leather with thick weave. Won't stop a sword, but thorns won't pierce it. +3 to saves against thorn damage."*

**Effect:**
- +3 bonus to Dexterity saves vs. environmental piercing damage (thorns, spikes, etc.)
- Reduces piercing damage from thorns by 2 (minimum 0)
- Does not protect against weapon attacks

---

### Soft-Soled Boots - 40 GP
*"Silent steps. Creatures using passive Perception have -3 to detect you. Good for sneaking up on skittish prey."*

**Effect:**
- Creatures have -3 penalty to passive Perception to hear you
- No effect against active Perception checks

---

### Grappling Hook + 50ft Silk Rope - 10 GP
*"Climb, swing, improvise. Arena's got archways and pillars. Might need to get vertical fast."*

**Effect:**
- DC 10 Dexterity (Acrobatics) or Strength (Athletics) to throw and secure hook
- Supports up to 800 lbs
- Silk rope is quieter than hemp

---

### Net (Standard) - 10 GP
*"Basic hunting net. 5-foot square, ranged weapon attack to throw. Restrains on hit. Not as good as that special net in the arena, but better than nothing."*

**Effect:**
- Martial ranged weapon
- Range: 5/15 feet
- Hit: Target is restrained
- DC 10 Strength check to break free (action)
- AC 10, 5 HP to destroy
- Can only restrain creatures up to Large size

---

### Reinforced Net - 25 GP
*"Better than standard. Harder to break. DC 12 to escape, AC 12, 10 HP. More reliable for holding wriggly things."*

**Effect:**
- Martial ranged weapon
- Range: 5/15 feet
- Hit: Target is restrained
- DC 12 Strength check to break free (action)
- AC 12, 10 HP to destroy
- Can restrain creatures up to Large size

---

### Climber's Kit - 25 GP
*"Pitons, boot tips, gloves. Climbing speed doubles, advantage on checks not to fall. Get on top of those pillars fast."*

**Effect:**
- Advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks to climb
- Climb at normal speed instead of half speed
- Prevents falling on failed check by 4 or less (catch yourself)

---

### Healer's Kit (10 uses) - 5 GP
*"Basic medical supplies. Stabilize dying allies, treat wounds. Also works for stopping bleeding from thorn cuts."*

**Effect:**
- Action to stabilize a dying creature without needing Medicine check
- Can be used to treat bleeding condition (DC 10 Medicine check)
- 10 uses

---

### Spyglass - 25 GP
*"See farther. Spot the Hummingwyrm across the arena. Advantage on Perception checks at long distance."*

**Effect:**
- Advantage on Perception checks relying on sight at long distances (60+ feet)
- Requires both hands to use

---

### Signal Horn - 5 GP
*"Loud. Heard up to 600 feet away. Coordinate with your team across the arena. Or scare the Hummingwyrm—your choice."*

**Effect:**
- Action to blow
- Creates loud sound audible up to 600 feet
- Can be used for signaling or distraction

## Services

### Weapon Padding - 15 GP
*"Wrap your weapon in padded cloth. First hit deals subdual damage automatically—no risk of killing. Lasts the whole match."*

**Effect:**
- Choose one melee weapon
- First hit automatically deals subdual damage (knocks out instead of kills)
- Reduces damage by 2 on all hits (minimum 1)
- Lasts entire match
- **Perfect for:** Catching the Hummingwyrm without killing it

---

### Beast Lore Briefing - 30 GP
*"I know a guy who knows about Hummingwyrms. He'll tell you their behavior patterns, flight tendencies, how to calm 'em. Gives you advantage on first Animal Handling check."*

**Effect:**
- Learn Hummingwyrm behavioral patterns
- Learn optimal catching methods (grappling is safest)
- Advantage on first Animal Handling check during the hunt
- Learn about the death curse (forewarned is forearmed)

---

### Pre-Match Scouting Report on Rivals - 25 GP
*"Intel on your opponents. How many, what weapons, general tactics. Know who you're up against before the gates open."*

**Effect:**
- Learn number of rival gladiators
- Learn general equipment (nets, javelins, etc.)
- Learn one tactical tendency (e.g., "they coordinate with a beast handler")

---

### Thorn Salve Application - 10 GP
*"Protective ointment. Apply before the match. First thorn hit you take, you automatically succeed the save. Doesn't last long, but it's something."*

**Effect:**
- Applied before match
- Automatically succeed on first Dexterity save vs. environmental piercing damage
- Prevents first instance of thorn damage and poison
- One-time protection

---

### Speed Training Session - 20 GP
*"Quick footwork drills. Warm up those legs. +5 feet to speed for the first 3 rounds of the match."*

**Effect:**
- Pre-match training session (30 minutes)
- +5 feet bonus to walking speed
- Duration: First 3 rounds of the match
