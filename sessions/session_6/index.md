# Session 6: Hunt the Hummingwym

A high-stakes chase where gladiators must capture and hold a magical Hummingwym—but killing it means death!

## Quick Summary

**Objective:** Catch and hold the Hummingwym until Round 12 ends. Don't kill it—or you die.

**Party:** 5 PCs, level 3

**Key Features:**
- Magical Hummingwym (tiny, fast, fragile)
- Death curse for killing it
- Special net hidden in arena (grants advantage)
- Environmental hazards (thorn bushes, sand pits)
- Rival gladiator team (4 fighters + beast handler)
- 12-round timer

**Unique Mechanics:**
- Animal Handling to predict movement
- Grapple checks safer than attacks
- Passing creature between allies
- Bleeding damage from thorns
- Concentration checks to maintain grip

**Reward:** Hummingwym bonds with party as familiar/ally
