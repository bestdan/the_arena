## Key Mechanics

### Pygmy Wyvern Stats
- AC 17, HP 15
- Speed: 60 ft (Rounds 1-3), 80 ft (Rounds 4-6), 100 ft (Rounds 7-8)
- Movement: Random (1d8 direction each round)
- Death Curse: Wisdom save DC 15 or drop to 0 HP if it dies in your possession
- Fragile: Critical hits kill it, 10+ damage in one hit kills it
- Grapple DC: 16 (Athletics or Acrobatics)

### Special Net
- Location: East archway (Investigation DC 14)
- Effect: Grants advantage on catching attempts
- Found by: [PC name]

### Thorn Bushes
- Dex save DC 12 when entering or starting turn inside
- Damage: 1d6 piercing on failure
- Failure by 5+: Additional 1d4 poison + bleeding (1 damage/round until treated)
- Treatment: DC 10 Medicine check or magical healing
