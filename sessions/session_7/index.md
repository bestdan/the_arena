# Session 7: Blazing Shadows

A brutal test of positioning and prediction as five gladiators must survive the Scorching Sun while battling fire-born hunters in a deadly dance with moving shadows.

## Quick Reference

**Party:** 5 PCs, Level 4
**Difficulty:** Hard
**Environment:** Circular arena with Scorching Sun overhead, four orbiting moon-stones casting moving shadows
**Enemies:** 3 Fire Elementals, 2 Salamanders, 1 Efreeti Enforcer, 4 Magma Mephits

**Key Mechanic:** Shadow Movement
- Shadows rotate 30 feet clockwise every round (Initiative 20)
- Sunlight deals 2d6 fire damage per turn (no save)
- Focal point convergence every 4 rounds (all shadows meet at center)

**Special Feature:** Shadow Riddles
- 10 riddles (5 silent, 5 shouted) to predict shadow movement
- Amplifying Stones required for shouted riddles
- Tactical risk/reward: share information with allies or keep it secret
