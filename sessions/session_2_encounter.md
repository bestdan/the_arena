## Enemy Composition

**Total XP:** ~700 (Medium-Hard encounter for this group)

### Primary Threat

**1 Ogre with Giant Cleaver** (CR 2, 450 XP)
- **HP:** 59
- **AC:** 11
- **Speed:** 40 ft
- **Attack:** Greatclub (Giant Cleaver reskin) +6 to hit, reach 5 ft, 2d8+4 bludgeoning
- **Ranged:** Rock +5 to hit, range 60/240, 2d8+4 bludgeoning
- **Str:** +4, **Con:** +3, **Other stats:** Weak
- **Tactics:**
  - Charges biggest threats or tries to break through to the kids
  - Ignores environmental hazards (intimidating)
  - Can throw rocks if kited
  - Will use cleaver to make sweeping attacks

### Secondary Controllers

**2 Hyena Masters** (Thug stat block, CR 1/2, 100 XP each)
- **HP:** 32 each
- **AC:** 11 (leather armor)
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **Weapon:** Whip +4 to hit, reach 10 ft, 1d4+1 slashing
  - **Special:** Can attempt to disarm or trip (contested Athletics/Acrobatics check)
- **Pack Tactics:** Advantage on attacks when ally within 5 ft of target
- **Multiattack:** Two melee attacks
- **Commands:** Bonus action to command both their hyenas
- **Tactics:**
  - Keep distance, use 10 ft reach to control space
  - Try to disarm/trip dangerous PCs
  - Drive targets toward environmental hazards
  - Focus fire on weak targets (the kids)
  - Position to give hyenas Pack Tactics advantage

### Pack Hunters

**4 Hyenas** (CR 0, 10 XP each)
- **HP:** 5 each
- **AC:** 11
- **Speed:** 50 ft
- **Attack:** Bite +2 to hit, reach 5 ft, 1d6 piercing
- **Pack Tactics:** Advantage on attacks when ally within 5 ft of target
- **Rampage:** When hyena reduces creature to 0 HP, it can move up to half speed and make bonus bite attack
- **Tactics:**
  - Dash to surround isolated targets
  - Swarm the kids specifically
  - Use superior speed to harass and reposition
  - Finish downed opponents with Rampage

### Reserve Forces (Deploy if Needed)

**1-2 Scimitar Fighters** (Bandit stat block, CR 1/8, 25 XP each)
- **HP:** 11 each
- **AC:** 12 (leather armor)
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **Attack:** Scimitar +3 to hit, reach 5 ft, 1d6+1 slashing
- **Deployment:** Only if party is steamrolling
- **Entrance:** Mid-combat as "surprise reinforcements" for dramatic effect

## Environmental Hazards

### Wall Spikes (North side)
- Any creature pushed/thrown into spikes: **DC 12 Dex save** or **2d6 piercing damage**
- Creates danger zone for forced movement effects
- Great for Panache moments (shoving enemies into spikes)

### Quicksand Patches (2x 10ft diameter areas, West and East)
- **Difficult terrain** - costs 2 ft of movement per 1 ft moved
- Creature starting turn in quicksand: **DC 12 Str save** or **restrained** until escape
- Escaping: Action, **DC 12 Athletics check**
- Even if not restrained, half movement to traverse
- Tactical value: Can kite ogre through it, trap controllers, dramatic rescues

## Tactical Breakdown

### Enemy Strategy

**Opening (Rounds 1-2):**
- Hyenas dash forward to harass and threaten kids
- Masters advance to whip range, begin controlling space
- Ogre advances menacingly but doesn't overcommit

**Mid-Fight (Rounds 3-5):**
- Hyenas swarm, focusing Pack Tactics on isolated targets
- Masters use whips to disarm/trip, drive PCs toward hazards
- Ogre engages primary threats or tries to break through to kill kids
- If anyone goes down, hyenas use Rampage to finish them

**Endgame (Rounds 6+):**
- Hyenas likely dead or mostly dead
- Masters become vulnerable, may try to flee or surrender
- Ogre fights to the death, chance for dramatic finish

### Party Optimal Strategy

**Defensive Formation:**
- Gladiator anchors center with Shield of Righteous Martyr (+1 AC to allies within 5 ft)
- Rohm guards opposite flank
- Kids stay between them, use spear reach to attack without exposing
- Merryn takes elevated position or kites from range

**Offensive:**
- Focus-fire hyenas first (low HP, removes action economy advantage)
- Use environmental hazards offensively (shove enemies)
- Control the masters before they control you
- Kite/control ogre last, it's slow and can be managed

**Key Resources:**
- Gladiator's Martyr's Intervention (5/day) - save kids from dying
- Crowd's Favor mechanics - build up early on hyenas
- Merryn's archery - take out hyenas efficiently
- Environmental hazards - maximize Panache opportunities

## Scaling Options

### Too Easy?
- **Add scimitar fighters mid-combat** - "The arena gates open again! Two more fighters rush in!"
- **Ogre gets second wind** - When bloodied, regains 2d8+4 HP (once)
- **Masters coordinate better** - Both focus on disarming/tripping same target

### Too Hard?
- **Merryn goes nova** - She starts using Crowd's Favor aggressively, showing off
- **Snak interferes** - "Accidentally" drops caltrops/nets from viewing box on enemies
- **Hyenas flee** - After losing 2-3, remaining hyenas break and run
- **Kids play dead** - After going down once, they stay down (stable) instead of being targeted

### Kids Going Down?
- **This is expected** - Makes protecting them heroic
- **Martyr's Intervention exists for this**
- **Rohm will go berserk** - If kid drops, he gains advantage on all attacks until kid is stable
- **Crowd's Favor helps** - "The Chant Rises" can give advantage on death saves
